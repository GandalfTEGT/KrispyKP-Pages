Overview

The site is built as a central place for everything under the KrispyKP name. Rather than splitting content across disconnected links, it brings together live streams, videos, music releases, and event coverage in one consistent space.

The main focus is strategy content, especially around Command & Conquer and related RTS games, with room for original music, tournament broadcasts, and future community features as the site grows.
